Segregation Guide

  • D
    Dry Waste (Blue Bin): Paper, plastics, glass, metal. Should be clean and dry.
  • W
    Wet Waste (Green Bin): Kitchen scraps, food waste, garden waste. Can be composted.
  • H
    Hazardous Waste: Batteries, paint, bulbs, medicines. Dispose of separately and with care.

Did you know?

Kolkata generates over 4,000 metric tonnes of solid waste every day. Proper segregation can help recycle a significant portion of this!
